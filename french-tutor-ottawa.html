French tutoring in Ottawa

Strong French matters more in the capital.

In a bilingual capital, French opens doors, at school, in federal workplaces, and in daily life. Winny French gives Ottawa learners affordable online French tutoring from just $25 an hour, for core French, immersion, and exam preparation, starting with a free 30-minute trial.

Ottawa families often aim for higher French proficiency than the minimum, given the region's bilingual workplaces and strong immersion programs. Our tutors know the Ontario French as a Second Language curriculum used by Ottawa boards, and several coach older students and adults toward DELF, TEF, TCF, and TCF Canada scores.

What we help Ottawa learners with

  • Core and extended French homework and assessments for Grades 4 to 12
  • French immersion support when a subject taught in French gets hard
  • DELF and DELF Junior preparation for students
  • TEF, TCF, and TCF Canada preparation for immigration and Express Entry
  • Adult French for bilingual workplaces and confidence in conversation
